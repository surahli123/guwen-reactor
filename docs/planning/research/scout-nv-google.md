## Demand Signals — Novel→Comic/Manhua (Sub-angle A)

### Signal 1: Yuewen "漫剧助手" Launch (Oct 2025)
- Yuewen Group (China's largest 网文 platform, owns Qidian) launched one-stop AI漫剧 tool for creators
- Opened **100,000 high-quality IPs** for creator access; seeded ¥100M+ creation fund
- Tool covers: novel parsing → storyboard generation → character/scene gen → editing
- AI now covers **80% of 漫剧 production workflow**
- Engagement proxy: 6,000 new 漫剧 in September 2025 alone (2× June 2025 volume)
- Source: [东方财富](https://caifuhao.eastmoney.com/news/20251021130830283960360), [虎嗅](https://www.huxiu.com/article/4811537.html)

### Signal 2: Platform Land-Grab (10+ platforms competing)
- Douyin, Kuaishou, iQIYI, Tencent Video, Bilibili, 番茄, 七猫, Baidu all investing heavily
- Revenue share up to 90% offered to attract creators
- 阅文's 漫剧助手 + competitor 漫剧工场 = competing one-stop pipelines
- Source: [36氪](https://www.36kr.com/p/3561521373854597)

### Signal 3: Copyright Crackdown = Proven Demand + Risk
- Zhihu founder 周源 submitted 2026 NPC proposal: "AI 漫剧 infringe faster than rights-holders can defend"
- Government regulation: AI 漫剧 must file with authorities from April 2026 (持证上岗)
- Three infringement patterns: unauthorized adaptation, piracy redistribution, name imitation
- **Implication for product:** personalized on USER's own reading history = only adaptations of novels THEY read = sidesteps unlicensed-commercial infringement IF personal use framing
- Sources: [知乎](https://www.zhihu.com/question/2013698168039166947), [21经济网](https://www.21jingji.com/article/20260410/herald/055ad9a327c3eed4dc4a0aa93f0863f2.html)

### Signal 4: Western Webtoon AI Controversy (Reader Backlash Risk)
- WEBTOON (world's largest) declared AI "cannot replace creators"; banned AI submissions from 2025 Webcomic Legends contest
- Naver Webtoon AI work caused **reader subscription boycott** when revealed
- BlueLine Studio forced to redraw episodes after fans spotted AI backgrounds in *Knight King*
- CHI 2026 paper: "reverse-halo" — enjoyment drops measurably once AI label revealed
- South Korea: mandatory AI content labeling law (Jan 2026)
- **Implication:** Disclosure and personal-use framing critical; mass-commercial route risks backlash
- Sources: [Screen Rant](https://screenrant.com/manhwa-webtoon-ai-art-concerns-replacement-impossible/), [CBR](https://www.cbr.com/webtoon-controversial-ai-product-develop/), [Korea Times](https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/lifestyle/trends/20251106/webtoon-industry-seeks-ai-edge-amid-legal-ethical-challenges)

## Demand Signals — Novel→Short Video/动画 (Sub-angle B)

### Signal 5: Donghua Adaptation Pipeline Demand
- 60%+ of donghua IPs sourced from web novels; studios hunger for pre-visualized storyboards
- 2026 trend report: "年番宇宙" (annual universe) series exploding; studios producing multiple 26+ episode arcs
- AI pre-vis for donghua pitched as pipeline accelerant (not replacement)
- Source: [搜狐](https://www.sohu.com/a/974036937_549401), [36氪](https://36kr.com/p/3718239685145993)

### Signal 6: AI Short Video Tooling Already Exists (Whitespace Mapping)
- **Jenova Comic Creator:** novel → 200+ page visual narrative; character reference system; targeted at both western and Chinese creators ([jenova.ai](https://www.jenova.ai/zh/resources/ai-comic-creator))
- **漫剧工场:** one-stop platform (script → AI storyboard → AI character/scene gen → distribution channel); launched 2025
- **漫剧助手 (Yuewen):** same pipeline but with 100K licensed IPs pre-loaded
- **Gap:** none of these are PERSONALIZED — they don't know what the user has actually read. All are generic "input any novel" tools.
- Source: [知乎 11款漫剧工具](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/2000469156861089713), [jenova.ai](https://www.jenova.ai/zh/resources/ai-comic-creator)

## Whitespace Summary (What Doesn't Exist)

| Sub-angle | Existing tools | Missing piece |
|---|---|---|
| Novel → comic/漫剧 | 漫剧助手, 漫剧工场, Jenova | Personalized on USER's reading history; classical 古文 support |
| Novel → donghua/short video | Generic text-to-video (Sora, Kling) | Novel-structure-aware (chapter → scene graph); character consistency across episodes |
| 吐槽 / 解说 | Generic commentary video tools | Novel-specific: plot hole detection, trope recognition, user-reading-state awareness |

---

## Copyright & Risk Notes

- Commercial adaptation of novels (even AI-generated) requires IP license in China from April 2026
- Personal/private use framing offers some cover; publishing commercially = legal exposure
- Reader backlash to covert AI use is documented and severe (subscription boycotts, forced redraws)
- Authentic "personalized for what I read" framing differentiates from mass-production tools and reduces backlash risk
